This cake is nice and fluffy. It also makes a good and simple birthday cake, unless you want to put on icing.

Almond Pound Cake

  • 3 Sticks Softened Butter
  • 3 Cups Sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp Almond Extract
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 5 Eggs
  • 3 1/3 Cups Flour
  • 1/2 tps Baking Powder
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Milk
  1. Combine Butter, Sugar, Almond Extract, and Vanilla Extract with a Stand Mixer
  2. After it has combined, add Eggs in one at a time, combining before adding the next one
  3. In a seperate bowl, combine Flour, Baking Powder, and Salt
  4. In the stand mixer, Add flour mixture and milk gradually, alternating between the two
  5. Grease a bundt pan
  6. In the bundt pan, add your batter
  7. Preheat oven to 300℉
  8. Bake Cake for 1.5 Hours
  9. Cool in pan for 10 minutes
  10. Put cake on wire rack and cool completely
  11. Enjoy!
