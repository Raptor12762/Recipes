Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

  • 3/4 Cup Softened Butter
  • 1 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 2 Cups Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • 2 1/2 Cups Powdered Sugar
  • 12 oz (2 Cups) Chocolate Chips
  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Combine Butter & Peanut Butter with Stand Mixer, Mix Well
  3. Add Graham Cracker Crumbs to Base and Mix
  4. Add Powdered Sugar to Base and Mix Well
  5. Take your Base and spread it along the bottom of a 9X13
  6. Take a fork and Score top of Base
  7. Melt Chocolate Chips in a Microwave Safe Bowl
  8. Spread Melted Chocolate Chips over Base
  9. Cut into Bars when Chocolate is cool and semi-solid & enjoy!

I love Peanut Butter Bars. They are chocolaty. They are also really easy to make for a large group.
