Pimiento Cheese

Pimiento Cheese

  • 1 Lb Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 6.5 oz Diced Pimientos
  • Mayo
  1. Grate Cheese using a food processor and put cheese in large mixing bowl
  2. Add Pimientos (and juices) and Mayo and mix
  3. Keep adding Mayo to desired richness
  4. Transfer to a container and store in the fridge
  5. Enjoy as a sandwich or with crackers or in your desired way

Pimiento Cheese Sandwiches make a good lunch. You can add meat to the sandwich or you can have it as is.
