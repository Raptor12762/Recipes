Waffles

Classic Breakfast. I always loved helping make it with my Grandfather everytime my family and I went up to visit. Have I told the story about buttons?

Waffles

  • 1 3/4 Cups Wheat Flour
  • 1 TBSP Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1 3/4 Cups Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Cooking Oil
  • 2 Egg Whites
  1. Combine Flour, Baking Powder, and Salt in a medium mixing bowl
  2. After combining, Seperate Egg whites into a seperate bowl and put the yolk in the dry mix
  3. Add Milk and Oil to dry mix and combine
  4. Whip Egg Whites until stiff peaks
  5. Add Egg Whites and Mix
  6. Cook Waffles according to Waffle Iron
  7. Enjoy!
