Peaches and Cream French Toast

  • 1 8-oz loaf of French Bread, Cubed
  • 8 Large Eggs
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 24-oz or 2 15-oz Can(s) Sliced Peaches
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream

    Night Before

  1. Grease a 9x13 Baking Dish
  2. Arrange Cubed Bread in Pan
  3. In a large bowl, Whisk Eggs
  4. Add Milk to Eggs and Whisk
  5. Add Sugar and Vanilla Extract to Egg Mix and Whisk
  6. Pour Egg Mix over Bread
  7. Arrange Peaches over top
  8. Sprinkle Brown Sugar and Cinnamon over top
  9. Pour Heavy Whipping Cream into a pan and Cook until reduced by half
  10. Pour over Peaches and Cream
  11. Refrigerate for 8 Hours

    12. Morning Of

  12. Pull your Peaches and Cream out of the refrigerator
  13. Preheat the Oven to 350℉
  14. Once the oven has Preheated, Cook your Peaches and Cream for 45-55 Minutes
  15. Let stand for 10 minutes, cut, and enjoy!
